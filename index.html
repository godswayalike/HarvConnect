THE PROBLEM

Ghana loses up to $3B in produce every year

Fragmentation, spoilage, and opaque pricing cost every participant in the vegetable supply chain. Harvconnect replaces that with coordination, fair prices, and direct trade.

For Farmers

  • Forced to sell through exploitative middlemen
  • No way to reach buyers outside their local market
  • 30-50% of all harvest lost to spoilage
  • Price volatility drops fair market prices

For Buyers

  • Struggle to find reliable, consistent supply
  • High effort to source quality produce
  • Opaque pricing - no benchmarks by region
  • Logistics failures to coordinate delivery

For Transport Providers

  • Fragmented load-matching pattern
  • Poor coordination causes delays and spoilage
  • Empty return trips waste up to 50% of revenue
  • Irregular load demand from informal brokers
HOW IT WORKS

From farm to buyers in 4 steps

Harvconnect replaces fragmented informal trade with a coordinated, transparent process that creates value for farmers, buyers, and transport providers alike.

01

Farmers lists produce

Farmers upload verified listings - produce name, photo, quantity, units, price and product details.

02

Buyers discovers supply

Buyers search or filter listings by the kind of produce, price, quality metrics, and region.

03

Order & negotiate

Buyers send inquiries or place orders. Chats and management occur directly through the app ecosystem.

04

Transport & deliver

Nearby transport providers are matched to open jobs. Track deliveries live until safe dropoff points.
